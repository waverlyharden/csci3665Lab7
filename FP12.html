Meeting with Collaborator:We met with our collaborator this Wednesday (11/15) to go over the general goal of our project. We decided the main questions we would like to focus on is Kate Furbish's travel throughout Maine (visualized as a choropleth) as she was collecting plant specimens as well as considering the amount of collections by grouping genera with their respective species (visualized as a stacked bar graph). A few alterations we would have to do with our data is create a column of just the genus names as well as ensure the regional names for Main are somewhat the same name as ones we found in our json file for the Maine Choropleth. Additionally, we were hoping to use the Harvard data in addition to the Bowdoin data for more specifics like the genus names as well as including months, rather than just years, for a better understanding of when Kate Furbish collected her specimens. This would also need to be standardized in our dataset as some are written with hyphens while others with forward slashes. Also, in the harvard data, if we could figure out correct links. It might be nice in addition to add links to her illustrations for each genus.

I think our biggest issue for FP11 was ensuring our data is in a good working mode for us to begin prototyping with the choropleth and stacked bar chart. So, hopefully we can see if there's one of the two that we can begin with.
